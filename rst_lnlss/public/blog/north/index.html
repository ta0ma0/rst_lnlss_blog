written by rst_lnlss on

flatline

by Koyorin

Slag it all! Life's gone sideways again. Recently, my meat-sack got canned by some corpo scum. Fascist bastards. Forced to relocate to my own sector of the megacity, where even breathing feels like a death wish. Missed my flight 'cause the first plane was late. Frag it all. Had to score a new ticket for another bird, but my synapses were fried. Ended up booking a seat a whole month out. Corpo suits refunded the first ticket, small mercy. Now I'm jacked into sector North 59.931130.3609. Interviews ain't for a while, so I'm prepping before I hit the Sprawl. Ran out of meds and caught a nasty glitch. Two sleepless nights had me tearing through Night City like a malfunctioning drone. Finally scored my fix, crawled into my pod, and crashed for a full rotation. Maybe it patched me up, saved my ass even. Could've taken some damage though. Gonna need a full system scan, but that's for later. Right now, I need creds and a job hunt that won't fry my circuits. Not all's lost in this neon hell. Seems like the universe is throwing me a bone. Keep your optics crossed for me.

Thought of the cycle: Stay frosty and RUN.
